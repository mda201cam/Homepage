Mark Allen Homepage

This is a work-orientated web-site illustrating my structural biology research based within the Laboratory of Molecular Biology (LMB) at the Medical Research Centre (MRC) site on Francis Crick Avenue. I use both X-ray crystallography and NMR to solve the protein structures (above and below) using whichever technique is most applicable, preferably a combination of both. Hopefully some EM images and structures will appear soon.

This website contains information about current and past projects recently complete by me and collegues, and the publications resulting from them. There is a page of useful links to other websites, particularly to those programs that are useful to my everyday research. The pictures above and below are a montage of some of protein structures either completed by me, or which I have contributed towards, within the past few years.

This website also contains information on some of the projects completed during my 4 years as a post-doctoral researcher at the Structural Biology Centre, Tsukuba (Japan) and structures completed as a result of my work during my Ph.D at Cambridge University. The pictures below are collection of some of the protein structures that result from work in Japan and from my Ph.D.
